About KOHC

Healthy Employees. Compliant Businesses. Safer Workplaces.

Who We Are

KOHC is a specialized occupational medical practice dedicated to supporting the health, safety, and compliance needs of industries across the Northern Cape, including mining, construction, manufacturing, agriculture, transport, logistics, energy, security, and commercial sectors.


We provide legally compliant, efficient and professional occupational health services designed to protect both employers and employees — while ensuring full alignment with South African workplace legislation.

Our Leadership & Team

Medical Expertise

Kimberley Occupational Health Centre is led by an experienced Occupational Medical Practitioner with expertise across multiple industries, including mining, construction, manufacturing, agriculture, transport, and commercial sectors. Graduating cum laude in Occupational Medicine from Stellenbosch University, our practice combines academic excellence with practical industry experience to deliver high-quality occupational healthcare and medical surveillance services.


Our OMP has expertise to ensure compliance with the requirements of the Department of Employment and Labour and the Department of Mineral and Petroleum Resources (DMPR), while tailoring our services to the specific risks and regulatory needs of each industry we serve.


Under hands-on management and operational oversight, our dedicated clinical staff maintains efficient systems, strict confidentiality, and fast turnaround times for employers who value reliability and minimal operational disruption.
