About KOHC

Healthy Employees. Compliant Businesses. Safer Workplaces.

Who We Are

Kimberley Occupational Health Clinic (KOHC) is a specialised occupational medical practice dedicated to supporting the mining, industrial, construction and agricultural sectors in the Northern Cape.


We provide legally compliant, efficient and professional occupational health services designed to protect both employers and employees — while ensuring full alignment with South African workplace legislation.

Our Leadership

Medical Expertise

KOHC is led by an experienced Occupational Medical Practitioner with extensive expertise in the mining industry. We ensure medical surveillance meets the strict standards of the DMRE and the Department of Employment and Labour.

Operational Excellence

Under hands-on management and operational oversight, KOHC ensures efficient systems, strict confidentiality, and fast turnaround times for employers who cannot afford delays.
