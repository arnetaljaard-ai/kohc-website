PAIA Manual

Prepared in accordance with Section 51 of the Promotion of Access to Information Act

1. Company Details

Company Name: Kimberley Occupational Health Centre (KOHC)

Industry: Occupational Health & Medical Services

Location: Kimberley, Northern Cape, South Africa

2. Purpose of this Manual

This manual provides an outline of the types of records held by KOHC and explains how one may submit a request for access to these records in terms of the PAIA Act.

3. Records Held by KOHC

We hold the following categories of records:

  • Company Records: Financial, operational, and administrative records.
  • Employee Records: Internal staff documentation.
  • Client/Patient Records: Occupational health files, medical surveillance data, and Certificates of Fitness (governed by strict medical confidentiality and POPIA).

4. Request Procedure

To request access to a record, please use the prescribed Form C as published by the South African Human Rights Commission (SAHRC). Submit the completed form to our Information Officer. Please note that access to medical records is strictly controlled and may require written consent from the patient.
