# KOHC - Occupational Health Services

A professional web platform for KOHC, providing specialized occupational health and medical compliance services for the Mining and Industrial sectors in the Northern Cape.

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Mine Managers & Safety Officers (SHEQs)
- HR Managers & Industrial Directors
- Agricultural & Construction Lead Contractors

## 🎨 Branding Direction
- **Colours:** Charcoal (#333333), Steel Blue (#4682B4), Professional White (#F9F9F9)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, Compliant, Efficient, Partner-led.

## 🇿🇦 Compliance Focus
Built with South African regulatory requirements in mind:
- HPCSA Standards
- DMRE & Dept of Labour Compliance
- POPIA & PAIA Integration
