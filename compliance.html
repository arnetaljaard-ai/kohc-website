Mining & Industrial Compliance

Expert guidance to meet DMRE and Department of Labour standards.

Why Medical Compliance Matters

In high-risk environments like mining and construction, compliance is not just about ticking boxes—it is about protecting lives and shielding your business from severe legal liabilities.


Regulatory Frameworks We Support:

  • Department of Mineral Resources and Energy (DMRE)
  • Department of Employment and Labour
  • Mine Health and Safety Act (MHSA)
  • Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA)

The Cost of Non-Compliance

Failure to maintain proper medical surveillance can result in site shutdowns, severe financial penalties, and compromised compensation claims (COID).

How KOHC Simplifies It

We handle the medical bureaucracy. From audit-ready reporting to automated recall systems for expiring COFs, we ensure you are always protected and compliant.
