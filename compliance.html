Health and Safety Compliance

Expert guidance to meet Department of Mineral and Petroleum Resources (DMPR) and Department of Employment and Labour standards.

Why Medical Compliance Matters

In high-risk environments like mining, manufacturing, and construction, compliance is not just about ticking boxes—it is about protecting lives and shielding your business from severe legal liabilities.


Regulatory Frameworks We Support:

  • Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA), Act 85 of 1993
  • Mine Health and Safety Act (MHSA), Act 29 of 1996
  • Compensation for Occupational Injuries and Diseases Act (COIDA), Act 130 of 1993
  • National Health Act, Act 61 of 2003
  • Employment Equity Act, Act 55 of 1998
  • Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013
  • Department of Employment and Labour guidelines
  • Department of Mineral and Petroleum Resources (DMPR) standards
  • Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) ethical and professional standards

The Cost of Non-Compliance

Failure to maintain proper medical surveillance can result in site shutdowns, severe financial penalties, and compromised compensation claims (COID). Ultimately leading to decreased production.

How KOHC Simplifies It

We handle the medical bureaucracy. From audit-ready reporting to streamlined fitness certificates, we ensure you are protected and compliant.
