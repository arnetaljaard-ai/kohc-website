Privacy Policy (POPIA)

Protection of Personal Information Act Compliance

1. Introduction

Kimberley Occupational Health Centre (KOHC) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and medical information is collected and used properly, lawfully, and transparently in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process personal information mainly to provide employers and employees with occupational health services, including Certificates of Fitness (COF) and medical surveillance. This includes:

  • Identity numbers and contact details.
  • Employment history and occupational exposure data.
  • Medical history and clinical examination results.

3. How We Use Your Information

We will use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected, which includes:

  • Generating DMPR-compliant medical certificates.
  • Maintaining statutory medical surveillance records.
  • Communicating fitness-to-work statuses to authorized employers (HR/Safety Officers) while keeping clinical details confidential.

4. Information Security

We are legally obliged to provide adequate protection for the medical and personal information we hold and to stop unauthorized access and use of personal information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any queries about this policy or require access to your personal information, please contact our Information Officer via our Contact Page.
